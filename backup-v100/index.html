Platform Capabilities

More Modules. One intelligent platform.

A fully integrated pipeline — each module purpose-built and connected, from field sensor to decision-ready output.

AiOT Smart Data Collection

Surveys roads with accurate and precise multi-sensor capture — imagery, GPS, and lane geometry. No road closure required.

AI-Enhanced Defect Analysis

Classifies potholes, cracks, raveling, alligator cracking, spalling and more on AC and PCC surfaces, with GPS coordinates and mm dimensions per detection.

Road Performance & Digital Mapping

GIS maps overlay distress severity on live road networks. Heat maps, colour-coded condition bands, and GPS-linked defect markers.

Pavement Condition Evaluation

Automated road performance computation based on international (e.g. ASTM) and local standards — deduct value analysis, CDV, strip charts, and condition-band breakdowns.

Predictive Performance Modelling

ML models forecast pavement deterioration based on mechanistic empirical methods, enabling proactive maintenance planning before condition failure.

Budgeting Analysis & Optimisation

Shifts authorities from reactive repair to preventive management, significantly reducing total lifecycle cost.

Digital Twin Solution

A virtual replica of the road network, synchronised with real survey data, simulating maintenance interventions.
