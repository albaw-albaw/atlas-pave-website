The PAGI Initiative

The team behind
ATLAS.Pave

ATLAS.Pave is a product of the PAGI Initiative — a multidisciplinary research and engineering group combining expertise in artificial intelligence, pavement engineering, geographic information systems, and infrastructure asset management. It is supported by various authorities and leading property developers. Founded in Singapore, PAGI brings together practitioners with decades of hands-on experience in road construction, maintenance, and digital infrastructure across government and private sectors.

Our solutions bridge the gap between academic research and real-world deployment — producing solutions that are not just technically robust, but operationally practical. ATLAS.Pave is built on proven, peer-reviewed methodology. Every component, from the AiOT sensor protocol to the International (e.g. ASTM) and Local Standards, has been developed and validated through real survey campaigns across multiple countries and surface types.
